About Ballot Site

About

This Ballot site was created to facilitate fair, transparent, and anonymous allocation of limited resources, tasks, or prizes among a defined group of users. Our system guarantees that each registered user can select **one and only one** private ballot during an active session, ensuring a level playing field for everyone.

Integrity and Fairness

Built by the Engineering team at iCISTEM, the core principle of this application is fairness. Once a user logs in and picks a ballot, that selection is immediately and atomically recorded in the database. This prevents any other user from selecting the same ballot, even in a high-traffic moment. The single-pick rule is enforced across devices and future logins using secure session management and persistent database checks.

Technology Stack

Developed by the engineering team at iCISTEM, the core tools are

  • Frontend: HTML/CSS with Jinja templating.
  • Backend: Flask (Python).
  • Database: MongoDB, leveraging atomic operations for ballot selection integrity.
  • Deployment: Render, ensuring global accessibility and stable hosting.
